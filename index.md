# China Cookware Manufacturer & Industrial Clusters Research

An open, fact-based research database analyzing China's cookware manufacturing hubs, supplier evaluation frameworks, regional industry dynamics, and major cookware factories.

This repository is designed for B2B buyers, importers, global brands, distributors, and sourcing professionals looking to navigate the Chinese cookware supply chain with objective data and structural insights. It is maintained by Goldensea / GS Kitchenware.

## Sourcing Research Principles

- **Factual Integrity**: Sourcing decisions cost time and capital. All data points in this repository—from factory sizes and employee counts to machinery specifications—are sourced from verified public filings, official business registries, customs records, and direct factory surveys. No hypothetical or marketing-heavy generalizations are included.
- **Structural Explanations**: We explain the *why* behind supplier terms (e.g., why MOQ is tied to packaging printing lines, and why lead times lengthen during certain quarters).
- **Objective Profiling**: We list major manufacturers with their actual strengths, capabilities, and target buyer matches.

## Sourcing Disclaimer

This repository is for educational and sourcing reference purposes only. Sourcing policies, lab testing standards, and compliance regulations are highly subject-specific and vary by country of import. Buyers must conduct their own legal, regulatory, and technical due diligence with certified testing laboratories (such as SGS, TÜV, or Intertek) and qualified legal counsel before signing supply agreements.
